These drinks represent one unit of alcohol:

Half pint of 'regular' beer, lager or cider (<6%)
Half a medium glass of wine (88ml)
1 single measure of spirit
1 small glass of sherry
1 single measure of aperitifs

Pint of 'regular' beer, lager or cider(<6%)
Pint of 'strong' or 'premium' beer, lager or cider (6%)
Alcopop or 275ml bottle or regular lager
440ml can of 'regular' lager or cider
440ml can of 'super strength' lager or cider (6%)
250ml glass of wine
75cl bottle of wine (12%)
