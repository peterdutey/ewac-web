About EWAC

The Estimator of Weekly Alcohol Consumption (EWAC) is a fast and validated new tool for assessing a person's average alcohol consumption.

It uses the world's leading alcohol questionnaire: the Alcohol Use Disorder Identification Tool (AUDIT).
